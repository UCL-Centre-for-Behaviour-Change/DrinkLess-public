Building a picture of yourself that is not based around drinking is an important part of changing your drinking.

The ‘Flipsides of drinking’ provide some examples of the negatives about drinking too much, which you may not have thought about. By drinking less, you can avoid these and follow your true values.

You can use ‘Memos’ to record messages to yourself in the future. This is a chance to record a motivational message about your true self and why you think you should limit your drinking.

The ‘I am’ section is about thinking about what is important to YOU. And then thinking about how drinking too much can affect whether you live up to your ideals.
