For more support...

If you find it difficult to cut down your drinking, or think you may have a problem with your drinking then we advise that you get in touch with your GP. They will be able to provide confidential advice and refer you for extra support.

You can find more information about getting support here.

You can also find support over the phone:

  • Drinkline is a free confidential helpline for people concerned about their, or someone else's, drinking. Call 0300 123 1110 (weekdays 9am–8pm, weekends 11am–4pm).
  • Alcoholics Anonymous, whose helpline is open 24/7 on 0800 9177 650. If you would prefer, you can also email them at help@aamail.org or live chat via their website at www.alcoholics-anonymous.org.uk.
