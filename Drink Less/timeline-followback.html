Timeline Follow-back

A couple of techniques can help you remember the drinks you had a few days ago. Use your diary to remind yourself of where you were and what you were doing on certain days; that often triggers memories of any drinks you had. Or if you’ve got regular drinking patterns these can help you recall your drinking. For example, you may go out for drinks after work each Thursday, meet friends on Fridays, or have wine with a meal on Saturdays (and Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays!).Â 

Try to be as accurate as you can, the better you can recall your drinking the more we can help you reduce it. The best way of all is to set the daily reminder to on (it’s in the Help section) and complete your drinking diary at a regular time each day.
