How to set good goals

Good goals are specific and a little challenging. Not too challenging though, because the hardest thing about making a change is often sticking with it. So it's important to keep your goals realistic. You can alter them at any time if you find they're too difficult or too easy%@.

You can set goals for:

  1. Number of alcohol free days a week
  2. The total amount of alcohol you drink
  3. The number of calories you get from alcohol
  4. The money you spend on alcohol.

We suggest you start with a couple and take it from there.
